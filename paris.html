Welcome to Paris

Paris, the city of lights, is known for its art, fashion, gastronomy, and culture. It's a global center for art and fashion and is home to iconic landmarks.

Must-See Attractions

Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower is a wrought-iron lattice tower on the Champ de Mars in Paris. It is named after the engineer Gustave Eiffel, whose company designed and built the tower.

Louvre Museum

The Louvre is the world's largest art museum and a historic monument in Paris, France. It is famous for housing works like the Mona Lisa and the Venus de Milo.

Notre-Dame Cathedral

Notre-Dame de Paris is a medieval Catholic cathedral on the Île de la Cité in Paris. It is widely considered one of the finest examples of French Gothic architecture.
